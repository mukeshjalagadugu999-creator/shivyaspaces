Trust & Security

Data Privacy & Security

Effective Date: June 2026  ·  Shivya Spaces

At Shivya Spaces, we understand that property information, tenant records, rental documents, and owner data are sensitive and confidential. Protecting your information is a core part of our platform design and operating principles. We are committed to maintaining the privacy, security, and integrity of the information entrusted to us.

We Protect Property Data

Property information uploaded to Shivya Spaces remains under the control of the property owner. We take reasonable measures to protect:

  • Property details
  • Rental information
  • Property images
  • Availability information
  • Rental documents and records

Only information intentionally published by the owner is made visible to prospective tenants through the platform.

We Protect Tenant Information

Tenant information is treated as confidential and is only accessible to authorized parties involved in the rental process. This may include:

  • Tenant profiles
  • Contact details
  • Rental applications
  • Onboarding information
  • Rental history records maintained within the platform

Tenant information is never publicly displayed beyond the purpose for which it was provided.

We Protect Owner Information

Owner information is safeguarded and is not shared publicly unless specifically authorized by the owner. Protected information may include:

  • Personal contact details
  • Property ownership records
  • Verification documents
  • Rental management information
  • Operational records and communications

Shivya Spaces does not sell owner information to third parties.

Cross-Owner Visibility Is Restricted

One of the core principles of Shivya Spaces is owner privacy. Property owners cannot:

  • View other owners' dashboards
  • Access other owners' properties
  • View other owners' tenants
  • Access other owners' agreements or operational records

Each owner operates within their own dedicated and isolated workspace.

Secure Authentication

Access to Shivya Spaces requires authenticated user credentials. We implement security measures designed to help protect user accounts and prevent unauthorized access. Users are encouraged to:

  • Use strong passwords
  • Keep login credentials confidential
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

Role-Based Access Controls

Access to information within Shivya Spaces is controlled based on operational requirements. Access may be limited according to roles such as:

  • Property Owner
  • Tenant
  • Platform Support
  • Verification Team
  • Platform Administrator

This helps ensure users only access information necessary for their role and responsibilities.

Encrypted Communications

Shivya Spaces uses industry-standard encryption technologies to help protect information transmitted between users and our platform. This includes:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Protected data transmission channels

Encryption helps safeguard information from unauthorized interception during transmission.

Administrative Access

As with most modern cloud platforms, authorized Shivya Spaces administrators may access certain information when necessary for:

  • Customer support
  • Technical troubleshooting
  • Security monitoring
  • Verification processes
  • Legal or regulatory compliance

Such access is limited, controlled, and used only for legitimate operational purposes.

Continuous Improvement

Security and privacy are ongoing commitments. As Shivya Spaces grows, we will continue to enhance our platform through:

  • Improved security controls
  • Enhanced monitoring capabilities
  • Stronger access management practices
  • Additional privacy safeguards
  • Security best-practice adoption

Our Commitment

Our goal is to provide NRI property owners and tenants with a trusted digital platform where rental operations can be managed securely, efficiently, and confidently. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and responsible use of the information entrusted to us.
