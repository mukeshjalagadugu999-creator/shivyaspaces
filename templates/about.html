About Us

Shivya Spaces — Trusted Rental Lifecycle Platform for NRI Property Owners

Shivya Spaces is a trusted rental lifecycle platform built to simplify property management for NRI property owners managing rental homes remotely in India. Managing rental properties from abroad often comes with challenges — dependence on brokers, lack of transparency, tenant coordination difficulties, documentation hassles, rent tracking issues, and operational follow-ups. Shivya Spaces was created to solve these real-world problems through a modern digital platform focused on trust, simplicity, and operational visibility.

What We Do

Our platform enables property owners to:

  • Create their own branded property portal
  • List and manage rental properties
  • Receive tenant inquiries directly
  • Digitally manage onboarding workflows
  • Generate rental agreements
  • Track rent reminders and receipts
  • Streamline rental operations from anywhere in the world

Unlike traditional listing platforms that focus primarily on property advertisements and lead generation, Shivya Spaces focuses on the complete rental lifecycle experience for both owners and tenants.
